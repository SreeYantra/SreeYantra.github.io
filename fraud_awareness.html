Phishing Email

This is when scammers trick you into giving away personal information through fake emails.

Identity Theft

Cybercriminals steal your personal data to impersonate you and commit fraud.

Fake Websites

Scammers create websites that look real to steal your login information or money.

Online Scams

Scammers try to convince you to send money or give personal details for fake offers.

Ransomware

This is when hackers block access to your data and demand payment to restore it.

Social Engineering

Scammers manipulate you into revealing personal information using social tricks.

Fake Documents

Scammers create fake documents to trick you into making a wrong decision.
